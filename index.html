The Story

ViaMedia is a British-Norwegian band that writes, records, and performs music that is adventurous, creative, and seeks to avoid any form of orthodoxy. We are a post-prog / art rock band, deeply inspired by contemporary classical and cinematic music.

The band is a reboot of a popular Bergen-based band from the late 80s and early 90s, which was heavily influenced by acts like David Sylvian, Talk Talk, and Ultravox. Today's ViaMedia is far more adventurous and eclectic.

Founders Karl Yngvar Dale (Molde, Norway) and Tore Dimmestøl (Henley-On-Thames, UK) have collaborated remotely using Logic Pro, combined with recording sessions at John Mitchell's Outhouse Studios.
